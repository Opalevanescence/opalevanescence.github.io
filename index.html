About

Hey there, I'm Jessica and I'm always seeking out opportunities for me to continue expanding my technical skills, improving my leadership abilities, and making the world a little more accessible for everyone. On my teams, I advocate for more & better testing, clean & intuitive user experiences, and treating the code base like an owner, not a renter. When coding, I try to build solutions that center the user's experience, are easy to maintain, and are intuitively placed in the existing infrastructure of code.

I'm responsible, cooperative, growth minded, and seek out leadership and mentoring roles.

On the weekends you are most likely to find me exploring the world, playing board games, or working on my latest project (whether that be woodworking, wrting, or home improvments).
